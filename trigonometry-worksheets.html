Trigonometry Practice Worksheets

Basic Identities

  1. Prove that: \(\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1\)
  2. Prove that: \(1 + \tan^2 \theta = \sec^2 \theta\)
  3. Simplify: \(\frac{1 - \cos^2 \theta}{\sin^2 \theta}\)
  4. Prove: \(\frac{1 - \tan^2 A}{1 + \tan^2 A} = \cos 2A\)

Trigonometric Equations

  1. Solve: \(\sin x = \frac{1}{2}\)
  2. Solve: \(\cos x = 0\)
  3. Solve: \(\tan x = \sqrt{3}\)
  4. Solve: \(2 \sin x = \sqrt{3}\)

Applications

  1. A ladder 10 m long leans against a wall. The foot of the ladder is 6 m away from the wall. Find the angle of elevation.
  2. The angle of elevation of the top of a tower from a point on the ground is 30°. If the tower is 50 m high, find the distance of the point from the base of the tower.
  3. From the top of a 60 m building, the angle of depression of a car on the road is 30°. Find the distance of the car from the building.
  4. A man observes the top of a building at an angle of elevation of 45°. If the building is 20 m tall, find the distance from the man to the building.

Higher Level

  1. Prove: \(\frac{1 + \sin \theta}{\cos \theta} = \tan\left(\frac{\pi}{4} + \frac{\theta}{2}\right)\)
  2. Prove: \(\sin A + \sin B = 2 \sin\left(\frac{A+B}{2}\right)\cos\left(\frac{A-B}{2}\right)\)
  3. Simplify: \(\frac{\tan^2 \theta}{1+\tan^2 \theta}\)
  4. If \(\tan \theta = \frac{3}{4}\), find \(\sin \theta\) and \(\cos \theta\).

Mixed Practice

  1. Prove: \(\cos^4 \theta - \sin^4 \theta = \cos 2\theta\)
  2. If \(\sec \theta + \tan \theta = 5\), find \(\sec \theta - \tan \theta\).
  3. Prove: \(\frac{1 - \cos A}{\sin A} = \frac{\sin A}{1 + \cos A}\)
  4. Simplify: \(\frac{1+\sin A}{\cos A} + \frac{\cos A}{1+\sin A}\)
