Miscellaneous Practice Worksheets

These worksheets contain a mix of questions from various topics for revision purposes. Attempt them carefully and check formulas if needed.

Mixed Practice Questions

  1. Solve for x: \(x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0\) (Algebra)
  2. Simplify: \(\frac{\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta}{1}\) (Trigonometry)
  3. Find derivative: \(y = x^3 - 4x + 1\) (Calculus)
  4. Find: \(\int (2x + 3) dx\) (Calculus)
  5. A coin is tossed twice. Find probability of exactly one head. (Probability)
  6. Find area of triangle with sides 5, 6, 7 (Geometry)
  7. Prove: \((a+b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2\) (Algebra)
  8. Solve: \(\tan^2 A + 1 = \sec^2 A\) (Trigonometry)
  9. Volume of a cylinder with radius 7 cm and height 10 cm (Geometry)
  10. Probability of getting a prime number when a die is rolled (Probability)
  11. Solve: \(\frac{d}{dx} (\sin x \cdot \cos x)\) (Calculus)
  12. Simplify: \((x+1)^3 - (x-1)^3\) (Algebra)
  13. Find distance between points (3,4) and (7,1) (Geometry)
  14. Solve: \(\sin 2\theta = \sqrt{3}/2\) (Trigonometry)
  15. Find definite integral: \(\int_0^2 (x^2 + 1) dx\) (Calculus)
  16. Two cards are drawn from a deck. Probability that both are aces (Probability)
  17. Area of circle with radius 7 cm (Geometry)
  18. Solve: \(x^2 + 4x - 5 = 0\) (Algebra)
  19. Evaluate: \(\int \cos x dx\) (Calculus)
  20. Find: \(\tan 45° + \cot 45°\) (Trigonometry)
