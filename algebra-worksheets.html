Algebra Practice Worksheets

Algebraic Identities

  1. Expand and simplify: \((a+b)^2\)
  2. Expand: \((x+2)(x+3)\)
  3. Simplify: \((2x+5)^2 - (2x-5)^2\)
  4. Show that: \((a+b+c)^2 = a^2 + b^2 + c^2 + 2(ab+bc+ca)\)

Factorization

  1. Factorize: \(x^2 + 7x + 12\)
  2. Factorize: \(2x^2 + 5x - 3\)
  3. Factorize completely: \(a^2 - b^2\)
  4. Solve by factorization: \(x^2 - 9x + 20 = 0\)

Equations

  1. Solve: \(2x + 5 = 15\)
  2. Solve: \(x^2 - 4x - 21 = 0\)
  3. If \(2x+3 = 7\), find \(x\).
  4. Solve: \(\frac{1}{x} + \frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{4}\)

Word Problems

  1. The sum of two numbers is 27 and their product is 182. Find the numbers.
  2. A train travels 60 km at a speed of \(x\) km/h and returns at a speed of \(x+20\) km/h. Find the average speed.
  3. The length of a rectangle is 5 cm more than its breadth. If the area is 300 cm², find its dimensions.
  4. The sum of a number and its reciprocal is \(\frac{10}{3}\). Find the number.

Higher Level

  1. Solve: \(2x^2 - 5x + 3 = 0\)
  2. If \(x + \frac{1}{x} = 2\), find \(x^2 + \frac{1}{x^2}\).
  3. If \(\alpha\) and \(\beta\) are roots of \(x^2 - 7x + 12 = 0\), find \(\alpha^2 + \beta^2\).
  4. Simplify: \(\frac{x^2 - 4}{x^2 - 2x}\)
