Geometry Formulas (up to Class 12)

Triangles

  • Sum of angles: ∠A + ∠B + ∠C = 180°
  • Pythagoras theorem: In right triangle, a² + b² = c²
  • Area (Heron’s formula): √[s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)], s = (a+b+c)/2
  • Area = ½ × base × height
  • Equilateral triangle: Area = (√3 / 4) × a²

Circles

  • Circumference: 2πr
  • Area: πr²
  • Chord length: 2r sin(θ/2)
  • Sector area: (θ/360) × πr²
  • Arc length: (θ/360) × 2πr

Quadrilaterals

  • Square area: a², perimeter: 4a
  • Rectangle area: l × b, perimeter: 2(l + b)
  • Parallelogram area: base × height
  • Trapezium area: ½ × (sum of parallel sides) × height
  • Rhombus area: ½ × (product of diagonals)

Polygons

  • Sum of interior angles: (n-2) × 180°
  • Each interior angle (regular polygon): [(n-2) × 180°]/n
  • Sum of exterior angles: 360°
  • Each exterior angle (regular polygon): 360°/n

Coordinate Geometry

  • Distance between points: √[(x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)²]
  • Midpoint: ((x₁+x₂)/2 , (y₁+y₂)/2)
  • Slope of line: m = (y₂-y₁)/(x₂-x₁)
  • Equation of line (point-slope): y - y₁ = m(x - x₁)
  • Equation of circle: (x-h)² + (y-k)² = r²
