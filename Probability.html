Probability Formula Sheet

Basic Probability

  • Probability of an event: $P(E) = \frac{\text{Number of favorable outcomes}}{\text{Total number of outcomes}}$
  • Probability of not E: $P(E') = 1 - P(E)$
  • Probability of sure event: $P(S) = 1$
  • Probability of impossible event: $P(\emptyset) = 0$

Events

  • Union of two events: $P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B)$
  • Mutually exclusive events: $P(A \cap B) = 0$
  • Complementary events: $P(A) + P(A') = 1$

Conditional Probability

  • $P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}, \text{ if } P(B) \neq 0$
  • Independent events: $P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B)$

Random Variables & Expectation

  • Expected value: $E(X) = \sum x_i P(X=x_i)$
  • Variance: $\text{Var}(X) = E(X^2) - (E(X))^2$
  • Standard deviation: $\sigma = \sqrt{\text{Var}(X)}$

Miscellaneous Formulas

  • Complement Rule: $P(A') = 1 - P(A)$
  • Addition Rule for 3 events: $P(A \cup B \cup C) = P(A)+P(B)+P(C)-P(A \cap B)-P(A \cap C)-P(B \cap C)+P(A \cap B \cap C)$
  • Number of outcomes in equally likely events: $n(E)$ for event $E$
