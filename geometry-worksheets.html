Geometry Practice Worksheets

Lines & Angles

  1. If two angles are complementary and one angle is 35°, find the other.
  2. Two parallel lines are cut by a transversal. One angle is 70°. Find all other angles.
  3. In a triangle, if two angles are 45° and 55°, find the third angle.
  4. The bisector of an angle of a triangle divides the opposite side into 5 cm and 3 cm. Find the sides of the triangle.

Triangles

  1. Prove that the sum of angles of a triangle is 180°.
  2. A triangle has sides 5 cm, 12 cm, and 13 cm. Prove it is a right-angled triangle.
  3. In ∆ABC, AB = AC and ∠B = 70°. Find ∠C.
  4. In ∆XYZ, prove Pythagoras theorem when ∠Z = 90°.

Quadrilaterals

  1. Prove that the diagonals of a rectangle are equal.
  2. Show that the diagonals of a rhombus bisect each other at right angles.
  3. A square has diagonal 10 cm. Find its side length.
  4. In a parallelogram, prove that opposite sides are equal.

Circles

  1. If a tangent to a circle is 12 cm long and radius is 5 cm, find the distance of the point of contact from the center.
  2. Two tangents are drawn from an external point to a circle. Prove they are equal.
  3. In a circle, prove that equal chords are equidistant from the center.
  4. Find the area of a sector of angle 60° in a circle of radius 7 cm.

Mensuration

  1. Find the surface area of a sphere of radius 7 cm.
  2. The volume of a cube is 512 cm³. Find its surface area.
  3. A cone has radius 7 cm and height 24 cm. Find its slant height and curved surface area.
  4. A cylindrical tank has radius 3.5 m and height 10 m. Find its capacity in liters.
